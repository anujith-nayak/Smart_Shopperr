# Smart_Shopper


### Shop Smarter. Spend Better.

Smart Shopper is an AI-powered shopping intelligence platform that helps users plan purchases, compare products, identify the best-value deals, track spending in real time, and understand shopping behavior through actionable insights.

Built with a modern full-stack architecture, Smart Shopper combines intelligent price analysis, budget tracking, behavioral analytics, and AI-driven recommendations into a single premium shopping experience.

---

## Overview

Most shoppers focus only on price.

Smart Shopper focuses on value.

The platform helps users make smarter purchasing decisions by analyzing product quantities, offers, combo packs, budgets, and spending habits to determine which purchases actually provide the best value.

Whether planning a grocery trip, comparing personal care products, or tracking monthly expenses, Smart Shopper acts as an intelligent shopping companion.

---

## Core Features

### Smart Shopping Planner

Create structured shopping sessions before visiting a store.

Features:

* Create shopping plans
* Add products and quantities
* Set shopping budgets
* Organize purchases efficiently
* Track planned vs purchased items

Example:

* Oil → 1L
* Shampoo → 200ml
* Chips → 2 Packs

---

### Product Comparison Engine

Compare multiple products intelligently.

Smart Shopper evaluates:

* Product price
* Quantity
* Unit cost
* Offer value
* Effective price per unit

Supported offer types:

* Normal Pricing
* Buy One Get One
* Combo Packs
* Discount Offers

AI-generated recommendations:

* Best Deal
* Smart Buy
* Overpriced
* Savings Insights

---

### Combo Optimizer

Analyze whether combo packs actually save money.

Example:

Combo Package:

* Shampoo 200ml
* Body Wash 150ml
* Face Wash 100ml

The system compares:

* Combo purchase cost
* Individual purchase cost

Then recommends the most economical option.

---

### Live Shopping Monitor

Track spending while shopping.

Features:

* Mark purchased items
* Monitor remaining budget
* Detect impulse purchases
* Live spending updates
* Savings tracking

Smart alerts:

* You added 3 impulse purchases
* You exceeded your snack budget
* You saved ₹120 today

---

### AI Insights Dashboard

Advanced analytics powered by shopping behavior.

Includes:

* Spending breakdown
* Category analysis
* Savings trends
* Budget performance
* Impulse purchase detection

Sample insights:

* You overspend mostly on snacks
* Combo deals saved you ₹430 this month
* You stay within budget 82% of the time

---

### Shopping History

Maintain complete shopping records.

Track:

* Session date
* Planned budget
* Actual spending
* Savings generated
* Purchased items

Includes filtering and search capabilities.

---

### AI Shopping Assistant

An intelligent assistant that helps users make smarter decisions.

Capabilities:

* Product recommendations
* Deal analysis

* Budget suggestions
* Shopping behavior summaries
